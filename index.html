Why you need a imaganary friend

Having an imaginary friend can be a positive experience for many people, especially children. Imaginary friends can serve as a form of emotional support and can help children learn how to express their feelings and emotions. They can also help children develop their creativity and imagination, and can serve as a safe outlet for children to explore and experiment with different roles and scenarios. Additionally, children with imaginary friends often have better social skills and are more confident in their interactions with others.
